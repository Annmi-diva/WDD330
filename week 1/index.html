    Master mobile UX

    • Develop content from the smallest device up.
    • Don't look for what people tell you they want, observe their way of life, habits and what they do in a daily basis and look for what is challenging for them to see the opportunities to make think easier or better, so you are able to truly see what they need.
    • 3 important thinks every program or web page need to be:
      • Immediate - quick responses so users feel that they have the help they need.
      • Relevant - Satisfy user needs and provide what they want in an easy and fast way.
      • Frictionless - We are talking about not only fast loading but functionality. Few steps to get to the desire information for users.
    • While building apps or web pages we need to look at people's way of navigation, so we are able to design the navigation in a way that is easy and intuitive for people, so they can feel is fast and easy.
    • Mobile = simplification
